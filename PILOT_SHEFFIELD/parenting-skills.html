    Parenting skills

    Courses

    Parents who attend one of our courses or group sessions will be better equipped to help:

    • their child to succeed
    • to control his/her temper
    • to get on better with others

    Topics

    Topics include:

    • how children learn through play
    • praising and rewarding
    • setting effective limits
    • handling bad behaviour

    Further information

    All courses are free and are run by experienced professional staff with backgrounds in social work and/or education. We provide childcare and refreshments. If you would like to find out more, please contact your local MAST.
